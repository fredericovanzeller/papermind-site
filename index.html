Questions

Common questions.

Does it work without internet?
Yes. After initial setup, PaperMind works completely offline. The AI lives on your Mac.
What happens to my documents?
Nothing leaves your Apple ecosystem. Documents stay in your iCloud and are processed only on your Mac.
What Mac do I need?
Any Mac with Apple Silicon (M1 or later) running a recent version of macOS.
What documents can it read?
PDFs, Word documents, plain text, and scanned images. Photos and screenshots work too.
When can I use it?
Soon. Join the waitlist and you'll be the first to know.
Can it answer in my language?
Yes. Ask in English, Portuguese, Spanish, French or German — the AI follows your language.
