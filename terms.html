1. What PaperMind is

PaperMind is a document management application for macOS and iOS that uses on-device artificial intelligence to index, classify, and answer questions about your personal documents. All processing happens locally on your device. PaperMind is developed by Frederico Van Zeller, a sole developer based in Lisbon, Portugal.

You are responsible for maintaining backups of your documents. If you delete the PaperMind data directory or uninstall the app, your indexed data will be lost. PaperMind cannot recover it.

3. AI-generated answers

PaperMind uses a local large language model to generate answers based on your documents. These answers are generated by AI and may contain errors, inaccuracies, or misinterpretations. PaperMind provides source citations so you can verify answers against the original documents.

AI-generated answers should not be relied upon as legal, financial, medical, or professional advice. Always verify important information against the source documents and consult qualified professionals for decisions with significant consequences.
